Iceland week — Jul 13–19

All six together · Airbnb “Greenkey”, Laugavegur 40a, Reykjavík

🧳 Packing (Iceland July, 10–14 °C): layers, not heavy coats — light thermal or tee, fleece, and a wind- and waterproof shell. Beanie + thin gloves for the boat and evenings. Shoes with grip, swimsuits (three soaks!), sunglasses — daylight ~20 hrs. No umbrellas, the wind eats them. Forecast: vedur.is
Tue 14
Easy city day + Blue Lagoon 20:00 BOOKED
Hop-on-hop-off bus at whatever pace feels good. Evening: the famous milky-blue lagoon — indoor water entry both ways, warm all the way. Home ~23:15 in full daylight.

Amsterdam week — Jul 20–25

One nice thing a day. Cafés, canals, no alarms.

☀️ Typical: 14–22 °C, occasional short showers. Normal summer clothes + one light rain layer + very comfortable shoes (cobblestones + canal-side walking). The Iceland gear stays in the suitcase.
—
The gentle menu
  • Canal cruise (1 hr, covered boats) — best first-day overview
  • Rijksmuseum — Rembrandt’s Night Watch (timed ticket)
  • Van Gogh Museum — timed ticket, book a late-morning slot
  • Jordaan quarter — prettiest streets, coffee + apple pie at Winkel 43
  • Albert Cuyp market — stroopwafels made in front of you
  • Vondelpark — the city’s garden, benches everywhere
  • Bloemenmarkt floating flower market → Begijnhof courtyard
